# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who wants to contribute to or interact with the reptimeline project, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with reptimeline.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at **arturoornelas62@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The maintainer has the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors deemed inappropriate.
